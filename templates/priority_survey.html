Determine How Assignments are Prioritized:



Standard prioritization puts a 50% weight on the due date, 30% on your personal priority, and 20% on the assignment percent worth. Length of the assignment is not factored into priority



Early prioritization puts a 50% weight on the due date, 10% on your personal priority, 10% on the assignment percent worth, and 30% on the length of the assignment. The weight on the length is designed to make shorter assignments have higher priority.



Late prioritization has the same weighting as early prioritization except for the weighting on the length of the assignment is designed to make longer assignments have higher priority


Determine How the Scheduling Works:



Group scheduling means that work sessions will consist of one assignment until that assignment is finished.


Split scheduling means that work sessions will be split up with multiple assignments.
